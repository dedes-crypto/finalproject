Ethics & Legal Issues in Web Development

Introduction

Web development is not just about coding; it's also about understanding the ethical implications and legal responsibilities that come with it. This page discusses the key ethical issues developers face.

Understanding Ethics in Web Development

Key Ethical Issues

  • Data Privacy: Understanding how to handle user data responsibly.
  • Plagiarism: Acknowledging the sources of content and code.
  • Accessibility: Ensuring websites are usable for everyone, including people with disabilities.
  • User Consent: Obtaining consent before collecting or using personal information.

Legal Considerations

As web developers, it's crucial to be aware of laws that affect our work, including copyright laws and regulations on data protection like GDPR that govern user privacy.
